Why medicaid?

Medicaid is the largest health care program in the country – and growing – providing care to 70 million Americans, roughly 20% of the U.S. population. Across the country, 1 out of 3 children receives Medicaid/CHIP benefits.

Medicaid has become a proving ground for health care innovation, and the program has made huge strides in social gains, cost savings, and health outcomes.
